RANK SYSTEM BREAKDOWN

The ranking system currently consists of three separate groups: (Ⅰ) Lower Echelon , (ⅠⅠ) Central Assembly & (ⅠⅠⅠ) Upper Echelon.

You can progress through the Lower Echelon and Central Assembly ranks by attending both warfare events and assessments. Upper Echelon can only be obtained through applications or being chosen by the Council.

DRAGONBORN RANKS

I. LOWER ECHELON

Initiate: First rank of the group

Neophyte: Obtainable by purchasing the uniform in the group store

Ascendant: Obtainable by attending 2 total events

Sentinel: Obtainable by attending 4 total events

Crusader: Obtainable by attending 8 total events

Vanguard: Obtainable by attending 16 total events

II. CENTRAL ASSEMBLY

Legionary: Obtainable by attending 38 total events

Arcane: Obtainable by attending 76 total events

Blademaster: Obtainable by attending 124 total events

Dragonborn: Obtainable by atending 200 total events.

III. UPPER ECHELON

EMPRESS

The Empress holds absolute authority within Dragonborn, possessing the power to take executive action at any time as he sees fit. His authority is unquestionable and limitless, and he is directly responsible for guiding the course of Dragonborn as a whole and overseeing the Dragonborn High Command.

III. UPPER ECHELON

EMPEROR

The Emperor holds absolute authority within Dragonborn, possessing the power to take executive action at any time as he sees fit. His authority is unquestionable and limitless, and he is directly responsible for guiding the course of Dragonborn as a whole and overseeing the Dragonborn High Command.

COMMUNITY DEPARTMENT

The Community Department is responsible for fostering a vibrant and inclusive community by organizing engaging events, facilitating open communication, and promoting a positive and supportive atmosphere among the members.

JUSTICE DEPARTMENT

The Justice Department is tasked with the important duty of safeguarding Dragonborn from internal or external threats. They play a pivotal role in the integrity of the community, ensuring that nothing slips past the Skywatcher's eyes.

WARFARE DEPARTMENT

The Warfare Department is tasked with the important duty of continuous internal and external operations to feed the war machine known as Dragonborn. They are trained with the ability to properly host Warfare events, ensuring that each and every member improves and benefits.

PYRO FOUNDRY

As the Official Development Group for Dragonborn - PMC in Roblox, Pyro Foundry is a team of experienced developers dedicated to creating engaging and exciting gameplay experiences for players. The group is known for their innovative approach to game design and their ability to create immersive worlds that keep players coming back for more. With a focus on collaboration and creativity, Curators is always pushing the boundaries of what's possible in Roblox game development, and their passion for creating unforgettable games is evident in everything they do.

COVERT OPERATIONS

Covert Operations functions as a separate unit from the main group, serving as an elite task force specializing in raiding and defending all types of bases. Their singular goal is to bring glory to Dragonborn.

WYVERNS

Wyverns, Dragonborn's elites, represent excellence with unmatched skills, exceptional activity, unwavering loyalty and maturity. Serving as the backbone of Dragonborn, Wyverns not only excel with their skills, but also show strong discipline.

DRAGON GUARD

The Dragon Guard are those chosen out of the entirety of Dragonborn. These individuals have shown the greatest strides in activity, skill, loyalty, commitment, and devotion towards the group. They have been honored the greatest prestige and privilege to be in this division.

EXILES & PURGES

Those who are inactive for two weeks or longer without submitting an inactivity notice form will be exiled from the group, regardless of their rank. You can submit an inactivity notice through the #directory channel within the main Discord.

PROMOTION SYSTEM

Progressing through the ranks is done by attending assessments and warfare events. Similar to attendance points and the previous progression system, those who attend warfare events/assessments are given 1 XP each event they attend. This is the maximum amount of XP you can get per event.

DEPARTMENT INFORMATION

Dragonborn has two overseeing departments: Head of Internal Affairs and Head of External Affairs. They also have four regular departments: Warfare, Community, Development and Progression.

I. OVERSEEING DEPARTMENTS

Head of Internal Affairs: oversees all departments, handles justice/moderation.
Head of External Affars: handles diplomacy & outside relations with other clans.

DIVISIONAL INFORMATION

There are currently two divisions in Dragonborn that one can attain. These divisions consist of: Wyverns and Royal Guard.

II. WYVERNS

Roblox Group

Wyverns are the elite division of Dragonborn. They consistently display excellent traits of skill, activity, loyalty and maturity. Wyverns not only hold valuable amounts of skill but they're also disciplined, making them the backbone of the entire group.

To achieve Wyverns, you must be able to display the above traits for a substantial amount of time. Attending and performing well in assessments, and official events, receiving endorsements from Wyverns themselves and showing the traits listed in the presence of other officers will greatly improve your chances of becoming one.

III. ROYAL GUARD

Roblox Group

Established to win at all costs, the Royal Guards are considered the most prestigious and elite. These individuals have greatly excelled in skill, commitment, and devotion towards the group.
